About the Academy

Where Sacred Knowledge Meets Modern Learning

Light Upon Light Quran Academy was founded with a singular purpose: to make authentic Islamic education accessible to every Muslim family, wherever they are in the world. Our academy takes its name from the divine verse — Nūrun ʿalā Nūr — light upon light — reflecting our belief that Quranic knowledge illuminates the heart and guides every aspect of life.

We offer a comprehensive range of courses taught by qualified, experienced teachers who combine traditional scholarship with a compassionate, child-centred approach. From Tajweed and Hifz to Islamic Studies and Parenting Guidance, every programme is designed to nurture faith, character, and a lifelong love for the Quran.

6 Months

Tajweed Learning Course

The Tajweed Learning Course is designed for students who can already read the Quran but wish to refine their recitation according to the classical rules of Tajweed. Taught by certified Qaris, this programme provides a structured journey from the foundational rules of Makharij (points of articulation) to the mastery of Ghunnah, Ikhfaa, Idgham, and more.

Key Benefits

  • ✦ Master all 17 rules of Tajweed with practice
  • ✦ Correct Makharij (pronunciation points) of every letter
  • ✦ Recite with the beauty and melody of the Quran
  • ✦ Certificate upon successful completion
Enroll in This Course

Quran Learning Course

This comprehensive course takes students from learning the Arabic alphabet (Qa'ida) all the way to confident, fluent recitation of the complete Quran. The programme integrates Tajweed rules progressively so students develop correct recitation habits from the very beginning.

Key Benefits

  • ✦ Learn Arabic letters, vowels, and Noorani Qaida
  • ✦ Progress to fluent Quran recitation with Tajweed
  • ✦ Suitable for complete beginners of all ages
  • ✦ Regular milestone assessments and certificates

2 Years · Flagship Programme

Hifzul Qur'an Course

Our most prestigious programme — a structured, compassionate journey to memorising the complete Quran. Using the traditional Sabaq (new lesson), Sabqi (recent revision), and Manzil (older revision) system, students build solid, lasting memorisation under the guidance of specialist Hifz teachers.

Key Benefits

  • ✦ Full 30-juz memorisation structured programme
  • ✦ 4 sessions/week with specialist Hifz teacher
  • ✦ Sabaq, Sabqi & Manzil revision system
  • ✦ Weekly parent progress updates
  • ✦ Ijazah (chain of authority) certificate upon completion
